Iverson Answer V Campaign: Mobile Court Activation

Activation successful

GOAL:

We aimed to educate consumers and retail partners (Jimmy Jazz, Footaction, DTLR, and Superior associates on the Answer V campaign. By promoting sells through Iverson Answer V, we would be able increase brand awareness and visibility. The activation was a success with a post event sell through of 23 pairs across the four retail partners located in Cumberland Mall.

Consumers were given the opportunity to take shots on the court to receive Reebok basketball prizes and giveaways If consumers chose to purchase the Answer v they received a matching Answer V t-shirt and also a Iverson New Era hat. The event connected with consumers of all ages and demographics very well, and defiantly inspired consumers to check out future releases from Reebok.
